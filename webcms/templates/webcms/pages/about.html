Mission

Why we do what we do

A military man is first a servant to his allegiance before anything else, even their own families. His dreams and aspirations are an afterthought when it comes to service. Therefore, HDA has come to be a support to the children of late military personnel given that these fallen heroes were parents and every dream of a parent is the settlement and fulfillment of their children. HDA‘s aim is to shield children of fallen heroes from being victims of circumstances and proffer them an equitable footing to win in life.

Success Story

Sagesse de Dieu Limited is a renowned certified traditional carpentry firm offering an exquisite range of full luxury furniture and woodwork services. With a commitment to craftsmanship and attention to detail, we create exceptional pieces that enhance the beauty and functionality of any living or working space. At Sagesse de Dieu, we deliver unparalleled expertise and innovation in all our projects, catering to both residential and commercial clients. Our skilled team of master carpenters and artisans are dedicated to crafting custom-made furniture, intricate woodwork, and bespoke cabinetry that beautifully reflects your style and preferences. As a nationwide service provider, we cater to clients across Nigeria, and beyond bringing our expertise and craftsmanship to every corner of the country. Whether you’re in Lagos, Port Harcourt, or any other city, our team is ready to transform your vision into reality. For inquiries or to schedule a consultation, please contact us at 08099999369 Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will be more than happy to assist you.

In line with this vision, we have established Sagesse College of Traditional Carpentry, the first traditional carpentry college in Africa. Our college proudly trains and teaches both genders in the beautiful skill of carpentry, empowering them to become valuable assets to themselves and society. Through our comprehensive programs, students gain the knowledge and practical skills necessary to excel in this timeless craft. By providing equal opportunities and opening doors to individuals of all genders, we aim to promote diversity and foster a more inclusive society. At Sagesse College of Traditional Carpentry, we believe in harnessing the power of traditional trades to create a brighter future for Africa.
